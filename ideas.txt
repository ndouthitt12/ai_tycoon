[v0.04.0]

- Remove the cap of 99 for the Model Goals ratings. Change the values to be small input boxes that can be increased by using the down or up arrow.
    - There should be no cap for the player or the competitor companies.
- By default, increasing the values in the Model Goals should increase the development costs in the following way:
    - Speed should increase development costs by $0.5m per point + 0.5% of the total cost.
    - Accuracy should increase by $1.2m per point + 1% of the total cost.
    - Reasoning should have a $3.5m per point increase + 3.5% of the total cost.
    - Agentic should have a $5.5m per point increase + 4% of the total cost.
    - Coding should have a $1.8m per point increase + 2% of the total cost.
    - Multimodal should have a $4m per point increase + 3% of the total cost.
- These values should apply to competitor companies too.
- Add 10 competitor companies
- Researchers should reduce the cost of development by a random amount between 0.1% - 0.45% per researcher staffed. This amount should be random per model.
- Add an Admin tab where the player can do the following:
    - Add a custom amount to their current capital, in millions.
    - Adjust the cost per point of the Model Goals attributes, and the % of the costs for each.
        - These adjustments will impact competitor companies too.
